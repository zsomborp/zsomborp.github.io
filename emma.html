About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. Founded in 1902, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception. The word Real is Spanish for "royal" and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 together with the royal crown in the emblem. The team has played its home matches in the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium since 1947.
